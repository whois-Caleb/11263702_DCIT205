Department Research

Discover the forefront of innovation and exploration through the research initiatives at our Computer Science Department. Our faculty and students engage in cutting-edge projects that contribute to advancements in technology and address real-world challenges.

Research Areas

Our research spans a wide range of areas, including but not limited to:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
  • Cybersecurity and Network Security
  • Software Engineering and Development
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Distributed Systems and Cloud Computing

Current Projects

Explore some of the exciting research projects currently underway in our department:

  • Project Title 1: Exploring Explainable AI for Decision Support Systems
  • Project Title 2: Enhancing Cybersecurity Through Threat Intelligence
  • Project Title 3: Advancements in Natural Language Processing for Chatbot Development

Publications

Our faculty members and students actively contribute to academic journals, conferences, and publications. Some recent publications include:

  • Robotics and AI in Autonomous Vehicles: Challenges and Solutions - Dr. Amanda Mitchell, Robotics and Autonomous Systems, 2023
  • Human-Computer Interaction in Virtual Reality Environments - Dr. Samuel Atta, ACM Transactions on Computer-Human Interaction, 2019
  • Data Science Approaches for Predictive Analytics in Healthcare - Dr. Nana Yaa Serwaa Asare, Journal of Health Informatics Research, 2022

Collaborations

We foster collaborations with industry partners, research institutions, and other academic departments. These collaborations enrich our research endeavors and provide valuable insights into real-world applications.
