Computer Science Department Hackathon

Welcome to our annual Hackathon event! An exciting opportunity for students to showcase their creativity, problem-solving skills, and passion for technology.

Event Details

Join us for a thrilling 24-hour hackathon where participants will collaboratively develop innovative projects, learn new technologies, and compete for exciting prizes. This event is open to all students with a passion for coding, design, and creating solutions to real-world problems.

Key Highlights

  • Engage in hands-on coding and problem-solving challenges.
  • Collaborate with fellow students to build innovative projects.
  • Network with industry professionals and gain insights into the tech industry.
  • Win exciting prizes and recognition for outstanding projects.

Date and Venue

The hackathon will take place on the 31st of November, 2023 at UGCS Lab 3. Stay tuned for more details on the schedule and logistics.

How to Participate

Registration will open soon. Keep an eye on our website and social media channels for updates. Don't miss your chance to be part of this exciting and educational event!

Contact Us

If you have any questions or inquiries about the hackathon, please contact us at dcs@ug.edu.gh.

Tech Talk Series: Cybersecurity Trends and Challenges

Join us for an insightful Tech Talk on the latest trends and challenges in Cybersecurity. This series aims to provide students with a deep understanding of the evolving landscape of cybersecurity and strategies to address emerging threats.

Event Details

Explore the dynamic field of cybersecurity through engaging talks by industry experts. Topics will cover cybersecurity trends, threat intelligence, ethical hacking, and strategies for securing digital environments.

Key Highlights

  • Insights from leading experts in the cybersecurity industry.
  • Discussions on the latest trends and challenges in cybersecurity.
  • Q&A sessions to interact with the speakers and deepen your understanding.
  • Networking opportunities with professionals and peers interested in cybersecurity.

Date and Venue

The Tech Talk will be held on 14th January, 2024 at JQB Room 19. Save the date, and stay tuned for more details on speakers and registration.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the Tech Talk series, please contact us at dcs@ug.edu.gh.
