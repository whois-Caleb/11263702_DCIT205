Meet our esteemed faculty


Dr. Jamal Deen Abdulai

Head of the Department of Computer Science

Dr. Abdulai is a senior member of the faculty who has several years of experience under his belt and is one staff member who requires no introduction. He holds a Doctorate degree in Computer Science awarded to him by the University of Glascow, where he had previously completed his Master's degree after obtaining his Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Ghana. He has contributed in an impressive sixty-three scientific publications concerning his various research areas. He has also executed a number of projects including;

  • The automation of the Ghana Highways Tollbooth System
  • The Integrated Road Construction Contract Management and Accounting System
  • The Public Investment Management System

His interests include:

Wireless Sensor Networks, Photonics, and Information Security.

Email: jabdulai@ug.edu.gh

Mr. Michael Agbo Tettey Soli

Lecturer

Mr. Soli is a gifted lecturer who's knack for imparting knowledge as well as his approachability and aimable manner make almost indispensible to the computer science department as a whole, but especially to his students. Outside the lecture hall, he is a critical thinker and solutions engineer with over a decade experience in software engineering and algorithm design.

His interests include:

Graph Theory, Analytic Number Theory, Image Segmentation, Algorithms, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, SDLC, Java, NoSQL, Web Services, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, Databases, Financial Markets, Neo4j, Cyber Security, and Recommender Systems.

Email: matsoli@ug.edu.gh

Mr. Mark Attah Mensah

Lecturer and Course Advisor

Mr. Mensah is an experienced member of the Computer Science Department faculty. He has also held positions as an IT manager and general manager, leading teams in the technology departments of multiple organizations. His extensive experience with Data Science, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence, and his IBM certification make him a boon to the department. In the proffessional arena, he has completed several projects in the fields of Information Technology and Computer Science and is proficient with several industry tools, such as Jira, the Microsoft Azure DevOps board, GitHub, Bitbucket, Power BI, and Tableau. In addition to his extensive list of qualifications and accomplishments, he also serves as a course advisor in the Computer Science Department.

His interests include:

Data Science, Machine Learining, Artificial Intelligence, Project Management, and Student Mentorship.

Email: mamensah@staff.ug.edu.gh
